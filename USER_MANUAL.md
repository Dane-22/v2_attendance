## Welcome

### What is the JAJR Attendance System?

The JAJR Attendance System is a modern attendance tracking solution that uses QR codes to record your check-in and check-out times. It's designed to make attendance tracking quick, accurate, and hassle-free.

### Key Benefits

- **Fast Attendance**: Scan your QR code and you're done in seconds
- **Accurate Records**: Automatic time tracking with no manual errors
- **Real-time Updates**: Your attendance is recorded instantly
- **Easy Access**: View your attendance history anytime
- **Mobile Friendly**: Use the mobile app for convenience

### System Requirements

**For Web Access:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
- Internet connection
- Valid login credentials

**For Mobile App:**
- Android 8.0+ or iOS 12.0+
- Camera access
- Internet connection

## For Employees

### Recording Your Attendance

#### Using QR Code Scanner (At Branch)

**Time In:**

1. **Arrive at Your Branch**
   - Go to your assigned branch
   - Locate the QR scanner device
   - Wait for your turn

2. **Prepare Your QR Code**
   - Take out your QR card
   - Ensure it's clean and undamaged
   - Hold it flat

3. **Scan Your QR Code**
   - Hold QR code 10-15cm from camera
   - Wait for the beep sound
   - Check the screen for your name
   - Confirm the time displayed

4. **Done!**
   - Your attendance is recorded
   - You'll see a confirmation message
   - The screen shows "Time In" status

**Time Out:**

1. **Prepare to Leave**
   - Complete your work for the day
   - Gather your belongings
   - Go to the scanner device

2. **Scan Your QR Code**
   - Same procedure as Time In
   - System automatically detects Time Out
   - Your work hours are calculated

3. **Verify Your Time Out**
   - Check the displayed time
   - Confirm it's correct
   - You're done for the day!

**Tips:**
- Scan at the same location daily for consistency
- If you forget your QR code, contact your supervisor
- Report any scanner issues immediately
- Keep your QR code protected from damage

### Understanding Your Attendance Status

**Status Types:**

- **Present**: You clocked in and out normally
- **Late**: You arrived after your scheduled time (usually after 9:00 AM)
- **Absent**: You didn't clock in for the day
- **Half Day**: You worked less than full day
- **Leave**: You're on approved leave

**What to Do If:**

- **You're Marked Late**: Ensure you arrive on time. If it's an error, contact HR.
- **You're Marked Absent**: If you were present, contact your supervisor to correct the record.
- **You Forgot to Scan**: Contact your supervisor immediately for manual recording.

## For Branch Staff

### Using the QR Scanner Device

#### Daily Setup

1. **Start the Device**
   - Power on the scanner device
   - Open the browser or app
   - Login with branch credentials
   - Wait for scanner to initialize

2. **Verify Camera**
   - Check camera is working
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Test with a sample QR code

3. **Ready for Scanning**
   - Position device at scanning station
   - Ensure stable internet connection
   - Keep device charged

#### Scanning Procedure

**For Employee Check-In:**

1. **Greet the Employee**
2. **Ask for Their QR Code**
3. **Position the QR Code**
   - Hold 10-15cm from camera
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Keep QR code flat

4. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Listen for beep
   - Check screen for employee name
   - Verify time displayed

5. **Acknowledge Employee**
   - Confirm attendance recorded
   - Wish them a good day

**For Employee Check-Out:**

1. **Same Procedure as Check-In**
2. **System Automatically Detects Time Out**
3. **Confirm with Employee**

#### Handling Common Situations

**Employee Forgot QR Code:**

1. **Ask for Employee Name or ID**
2. **Record Manually** (if you have access)
3. **Or direct them to supervisor**
4. **Note the time for manual entry**

**QR Code Damaged:**

1. **Try scanning at different angles**
2. **Adjust distance from camera**
3. **If still not working, use manual entry**
4. **Report to HR for QR code replacement**

**Scanner Not Working:**

1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Restart the device**
3. **Check camera permissions**
4. **Contact IT support if issue persists**

**Multiple Employees at Once:**

1. **Ask employees to form a line**
2. **Scan one at a time**
3. **Maintain order and fairness**
4. **Don't rush the process**

#### End of Day Procedure

1. **Review Today's Scans**
   - Check all employees scanned in
   - Verify no errors
   - Note any issues

2. **Report Issues**
   - Document any problems
   - Report to supervisor
   - Note missing scans

3. **Secure the Device**
   - Logout from system
   - Power off if needed
   - Store device securely

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What if I lose my QR code?**
A: Contact HR immediately. They will issue a replacement QR code. Your old QR code will be deactivated.

**Q: Can I share my QR code with someone else?**
A: No, never share your QR code. It's unique to you and sharing it could result in false attendance records.

**Q: What if the scanner is not working?**
A: Notify your supervisor immediately. They can record your attendance manually until the scanner is fixed.

**Q: Can I clock in from home?**
A: No, you must clock in at your assigned branch location using the scanner device.

**Q: What if I'm late?**
A: The system will automatically mark you as "Late" if you clock in after your scheduled time. This will be visible in your attendance record.

### Employee Questions

**Q: How do I view my attendance history?**
A: Login to the web portal or mobile app and go to "Attendance History" to view all your records.

**Q: Can I edit my own attendance?**
A: No, attendance records can only be edited by authorized personnel. Contact your supervisor for corrections.

**Q: How do I get my payslip?**
A: Payslips are available in the system after payroll processing. Go to "My Payslip" section to view or download.

**Q: What if I forget to clock out?**
A: Contact your supervisor immediately. They can manually record your clock-out time.

**Q: How is overtime calculated?**
A: Overtime is calculated based on hours worked beyond your regular schedule. Check with HR for specific overtime policies.

### Branch Staff Questions

**Q: What if an employee doesn't have their QR code?**
A: Record their name and time, then contact your supervisor for manual entry or provide alternative verification.

**Q: Can I use my phone as a scanner?**
A: Yes, you can use the mobile app to scan QR codes if authorized.

**Q: What if the scanner shows an error?**
A: Try scanning again. If the error persists, restart the device and contact IT support.

**Q: How do I know if a scan was successful?**
A: You'll hear a beep sound and see the employee's name on the screen with a confirmation message.

### Administrator Questions

**Q: How do I add a new branch?**
A: Go to Settings → Branches → Add Branch. Enter the details and save. Then create a branch user account.

**Q: Can I export attendance data?**
A: Yes, go to Reports section, select the report type, and export in CSV, Excel, or PDF format.

**Q: How do I reset a user's password?**
A: Go to Admin Users or Employee section, find the user, and click "Reset Password". Provide a temporary password and notify the user.

**Q: What's the difference between "Draft" and "Processed" payroll status?**
A: "Draft" means payroll is being prepared and can be edited. "Processed" means payroll is finalized and payslips are generated.

**Q: How do I archive old logs?**
A: Go to Logs section and click "Archive". The system will automatically archive logs based on retention settings.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Is there an offline mode?**
A: No, an internet connection is required for all operations.

**Q: How often is data backed up?**
A: Data is backed up daily at 2:00 AM. Contact IT for backup restoration if needed.

**Q: What if I encounter a system error?**
A: Note the error message, what you were doing, and contact IT support with these details.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, the system uses industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure authentication, and access controls.

## Glossary

**QR Code**: Quick Response code - a type of barcode that can be scanned with a camera to quickly access information.

**Time In**: The moment you arrive and start your workday, recorded by scanning your QR code.

**Time Out**: The moment you leave and end your workday, recorded by scanning your QR code.

**Attendance Record**: A record of your time-in, time-out, and total hours worked for a specific day.

**Payroll Period**: A specific time period (usually weekly or bi-weekly) for which payroll is calculated.

**Deductions**: Amounts subtracted from your gross pay, such as SSS, PhilHealth, HDMF, and cash advances.

**Gross Pay**: Your total pay before deductions.

**Net Pay**: Your take-home pay after all deductions.

**Branch**: Your assigned work location.

**Admin**: System administrator with access to manage the system.

**Branch Staff**: Personnel responsible for operating the QR scanner at branches.
